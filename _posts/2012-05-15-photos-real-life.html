There's a reason people call certain photos "candid" - the vast
majority of photos we take are posed. I am guilty of having a "photo
smile." I do the same smile in every photo. A smile, I'm told, that I
never do when I'm not being photographed. Although I have almost 1,000
tagged photos of myself on Facebook, how I actually look in day-to-day
life is a complete mystery to me.

For these reasons, this video of people who think they are posing for
pictures is most excellent:
http://gawker.com/5910211/marcel-the-shell-director-surreptitiously-films-fri...

It shows how posed we are in photos. That even photos that are made to
look "normal" are taken of people who sit still for several seconds in
order to compose the ideal shot.

It's also pretty funny.

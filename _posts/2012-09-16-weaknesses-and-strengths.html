"While practicing, it is important to ensure that there are no weak keys. Typing speed is typically determined by how slow these weak keys are typed rather than how fast the remaining keys are typed." - http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Touch_typing

I came across this quote while trying to find the average WPM typing speed. This fact made me pause and think for a second. Perhaps a typing lesson that could also be applied to life?
